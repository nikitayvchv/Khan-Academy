Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

  • Time: 15 min.
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Slices of white bread 2 slices
Smooth Peanut Butter 2 tbsp
Strawberry Jam 2 tbsp
Unsalted Butter 1 tbsp

Step 1: Place the bread slices on a work surface and spread the peanut butter on 1 slice of bread and the jam on the other; set aside..

Step 2: Melt the butter in a large frying pan over medium heat until foaming. Add the 2 slices of bread filling-side up and cook until the bottoms are golden brown and the filling is warmed through, about 6 minutes.

Step 3: Using a flat spatula, flip 1 slice of bread onto the other to close the sandwich. Transfer to a plate and serve immediately.

Rice Crispy Squares

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 12-24 people
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 1/4 cup
Miniature marshmallows 4 cups
Rice Crispy Cereal 5 cups

Step 1: Melt butter in large sauce pan over low heat. Add marshmallows and stir until melted and well-blended. Cook 2 minutes longer, stirring constantly. Remove from heat.

Step 2: Add cereal. Stir until well coated.

Step 3: Using buttered spatula or waxed paper, press mixture evenly and firmly in buttered 13 x 9 inch pan. Cut into 2 x 2 inch squares when cool.

Mediterranean Breakfast Bagels

  • Time: 5 min.
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
DEMPSTER'S Sun-Dried Tomato Bagels 2
spreadable goat cheese 1/4 cup
baby spinach 1/2 cup
tomato slices 4
eggs 2

Step 1: Toast split bagel halves.

Step 2: Spread goat cheese over the bottom halves of each bagel.

Step 3: Top each with spinach and tomatoes.

Step 4: Cook eggs in a non-stick skillet until desired doneness and arrange over tomatoes. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and top with remaining bagel halves to make sandwiches.
